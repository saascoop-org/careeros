# Vision

## Why CareerOS exists

Professional identity is usually fragmented across résumés, social networks, repositories, certificates, publications, videos, portfolios and private documents.

This fragmentation makes it difficult for people to:

- understand the full value of their trajectory;
- reuse verified professional evidence;
- adapt applications to different opportunities;
- maintain consistent profiles across platforms;
- identify gaps and future development paths;
- preserve ownership of their own career data.

CareerOS exists to make professional identity structured, portable, explainable and reusable.

## Long-term vision

CareerOS aims to become an open, multilingual and interoperable operating layer for career information.

A person should be able to maintain one trustworthy professional knowledge base and use it to generate different career assets without repeatedly rewriting the same history.

## Desired impact

CareerOS should help people:

- represent their experience more fairly;
- make evidence-based career decisions;
- reduce repetitive application work;
- communicate skills and outcomes clearly;
- preserve control over personal career data;
- access career intelligence without dependence on a single vendor.
