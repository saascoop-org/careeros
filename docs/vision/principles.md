# Product and Engineering Principles

## Open source first

Core capabilities, interfaces and governance should be publicly inspectable.

## Human controlled

AI assists the user. It does not own the professional narrative or make irreversible decisions without review.

## Evidence driven

Every professional claim should be traceable to a source, document, project or user-confirmed statement.

## Privacy by design

The platform should collect the minimum necessary data and provide explicit controls for storage, export and deletion.

## Explainable outputs

Generated materials should expose why information was selected, omitted or prioritized.

## Independent value

CareerOS must provide value as a standalone product and must not depend on another SaaSCoop project.

## Vendor neutral

The domain and core application logic must not depend on a specific cloud, model provider, vector database or applicant tracking system.

## Modular and replaceable

External systems must be accessed through ports and adapters.

## Multilingual by design

Language must be represented as explicit metadata, not as an afterthought.

## Accessible and inclusive

CareerOS should support different career paths, educational backgrounds, countries and communication styles.

## No fabrication

CareerOS must not create false experience, certifications, results or references.
