# Maintainers

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- protecting the mission and principles;
- reviewing contributions;
- maintaining release quality;
- enforcing security and privacy requirements;
- documenting significant decisions;
- disclosing conflicts of interest;
- supporting a respectful community;
- publishing transparent project updates.

## Adding maintainers

A future maintainer should demonstrate:

- sustained contributions;
- respectful collaboration;
- understanding of project principles;
- reliable review activity;
- responsible handling of personal data;
- no unresolved conflict of interest.

The decision must be recorded publicly.
