# Governance

## Current model

CareerOS begins with a maintainer-led, contribution-friendly governance model.

This model is appropriate while the project is small and product boundaries are still being validated.

## Principles

- decisions are documented;
- contribution is open;
- maintainers are accountable;
- conflicts of interest are disclosed;
- funding does not grant control over the project;
- technical authority is earned through contribution and trust;
- governance evolves with the community.

## Decision categories

### Routine

Handled through issues and pull requests.

### Significant technical decisions

Require an ADR.

### Product scope decisions

Require an issue or proposal with rationale and acceptance criteria.

### Governance changes

Require public discussion and explicit maintainer approval.

## Future evolution

As the contributor base grows, the project may adopt:

- multiple maintainers;
- domain reviewers;
- a technical steering group;
- documented voting rules;
- contributor and maintainer succession.
