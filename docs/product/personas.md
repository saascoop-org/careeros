# Personas

## Primary persona — International applicant

A professional seeking international or remote opportunities who needs to translate a broad career history into a concise, role-specific application.

### Needs

- reusable career information;
- English-language materials;
- ATS compatibility;
- evidence-based claims;
- fast tailoring for each opportunity;
- control over sensitive information.

## Secondary persona — Career advisor

A coach, mentor or advisor helping a professional improve positioning while preserving authorship and consent.

## Secondary persona — Open source contributor

A developer, designer, writer, researcher or HR specialist contributing to connectors, templates, domain rules, translations or documentation.

## Future persona — Organization

An organization using CareerOS-compatible schemas to help members maintain portable professional records.
